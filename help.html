1. Start the Game: Begin by pressing the "Start" button. You'll be presented with a grid of mysterious boxes.
  2. Make a Guess: Click on any box to reveal what's inside. If you find a gem, you've won! If not, you'll receive a clue to help guide your next guess.
  3. Time & Attempts: You have a limited number of guesses and a ticking clock. Use your guesses wisely and try to find the gem before time runs out.
  4. Winning: If you find the hidden gem before your guesses or time runs out, you win! Otherwise, you'll get a chance to try again.
  5. Levels: As you progress, levels become more challenging with more boxes and fewer hints. Can you master all levels and find the secret gem every time?
  6. info: you must not click more than six box without finding the secret game then the game will be over
  7. Good luck, and may you uncover all the Secret Gems!
